EN:
In this first level I focus on building my Python fundamentals through concrete, hands‑on exercises. I implement programs around variables and operators (such as calculating a shopping total and computing my age in the future), if/else practice for age categories and numeric‑to‑letter grades, and several loop exercises that print ranges of numbers, sum even values, and repeatedly take user input. I also practice working with lists and dictionaries to store simple data, write basic functions, and complete three mini‑projects: a grade‑average calculator, a number‑guessing game, and a terminal to‑do list, then refactor at least one of them into cleaner, function‑based code. I treat this checklist as the completion criteria for Level 1 before moving on to algorithms and data structures.
